Q1 WHAT IS REPLICATING OBJECTS IN AWS HOW ITS USEFUL?

ANS Replication objects in AWS is copying data from one AWS region ir bucket to another. It is useful when it comes to data redundancy, disaster recovery, low latency access, load balancing, data migration, and more.
	

Q2 How to setup cross-region replication in aws?

ANS To setup cross-region replication in AWS go to management, click replication, add all content and status enable, click replicate any encrypted object in AWS, select destination bucket, choose IAM.
